
**Introduction**

A **join** lets us combine rows from two or more tables, based on a related column.

There are several types of joins in SQL, each serving a different purpose:

- `JOIN`
- `LEFT JOIN`
- `RIGHT JOIN`
- `FULL JOIN`
- `UNION`
